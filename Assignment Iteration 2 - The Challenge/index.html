The Challenge

  • Originality: The game's point values are directly connected to the amount the player moves the cursor. If the player decides to stay safe and not move the cursor, they won't make any points, and will probably die.
  • Technical Merit: The game simply makes use of the position of the cursor. It randomly generates all of the "bullets" and places them at the edges of the game. with a specified velocity to move into the player's view with. The bullets are then destroyed when they reach the bounds of the world, only to be replaced by new bullets.
