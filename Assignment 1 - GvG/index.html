Don't touch the lava!

  • Originality: The game's point values are directly connected to the amount the player moves the cursor. If the player decides to stay safe and not move the cursor, they won't make any points.
  • Technical Merit: The game simply makes use of the position of the cursor. It randomly generates all of the "lava blocks" and places them around the game.
